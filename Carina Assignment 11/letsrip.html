LET'S RIP!

Highlights

  • Get your adrenaline pumping on a quad biking adventure
  • Boldly explore the Atlantis Dunes a mere 30 minutes from Cape Town CBD
  • Benefit from the expertise of an experienced instructor and guide
  • Learn how to safely travel up and down sands of dunes up to 50 meters high
  • Marvel at views of Table Mountain while riding the crest of the dunes

Full Description

Revel in soft sand dunes on the back of a quad bike or pipe car also known as a sidewinder on this outdoor adventure in the Atlantis Dunes just north of Cape Town.

Just a short drive up the picturesque West Coast of South Africa you'll find the Atlantic Dunes in Dassenberg, Atlantis. An important conservation area protecting the Atlantis Aquifer and native wildlife, the reserve is also home to some of the best spots for quad biking with dunes up to 50 meters high.

Check-in at the Parks office once you arrive and meet your instructor and guide, who will assist you with obtaining your parks permit (to be paid in cash). Once the paperwork has been handled, your instructor will take you through an introduction to quad biking and a thorough safety briefing.

Now that you're comfortable with the quad bike, follow your guide out into the dunes to enjoy an hour-long, adrenaline-pumping ride in the soft sands. Explore the dunes and marvel at the surrounding landscape, including views of Table Mountain as you complete your adventure.
